Project Overview. This project analyzes hystorical transactional data for Bright coffee shop to provide actionable insightsfor the newly appointed CEO .It focuses on revenue enhancement and product strategy optimization.

Objectives -Identify top revenue generating products -Determine peak sales periodsand trends across product types and time intervals. -Develop data driven recommendations to boost sales perfomance. Methodology -Data processing(Databricks) -created transaction time buckets (4-hour intervals) -created spend buckets from highest to lowest spender.

Data Analysis(Excel,Tableau) -created pivort tables and charts highlighting : -revenue by product category -best or worst selling products and product details -Peak sales intervals Presentation -Delivered an executive presentation with : -key takeaways -visuals -recommendations for sales growth -next steps(automation,multi location tracking)

Key recommendations -Launch target marketing campaigns during low sales periods, -increase inventory of top selling products -offer promotions of underperforming products -implement loyalty programs tied to peak customer times.

tools used -Databricks(data processing) -Excel and Tableau(data analysis) -Powerpoint (Presentation)
